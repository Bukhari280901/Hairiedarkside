My Journey as a Darkside

A path through shadow, struggle, and self-discovery

The Beginning

Every story has a turning point — a moment where the light dims and the unknown calls. Mine started quietly, with questions no one around me seemed to be asking.

The Descent

Walking into the dark wasn't about giving up on goodness — it was about confronting the parts of myself I had ignored: doubt, anger, ambition, and the hunger to understand power.

Lessons in the Shadows

The darkside taught me resilience. It showed me that fear and strength are often the same thing, just seen from different angles. I learned to embrace complexity instead of forcing simplicity.

Where I Stand Now

I no longer see light and dark as enemies. They are companions — both necessary for balance. My journey continues, shaped by both the shadows I've walked through and the light I still seek.
